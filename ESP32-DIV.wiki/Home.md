# ESP32-DIV Wiki

Welcome to the official Wiki for **ESP32-DIV**, a powerful open-source multi-band wireless toolkit built on the ESP32 microcontroller! Designed for wireless testing, signal analysis, and protocol exploration, the ESP32-DIV supports Wi-Fi, Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), 2.4GHz, and Sub-GHz frequencies. Whether you're a cybersecurity enthusiast, a wireless tech hobbyist, or an IoT developer, this project offers a versatile platform for your experiments.
